Info

Cookie Clicker is a game where you earn cookies by clicking on a cookie, with those cookies you can buy upgrades and buildings that automatically generate more cookies for you.

Upgrades

  • Cursor: +1 cookies per click and 1 cookie per second
  • Multiplier: +2 cookies per click
  • Super Click: +10 cookies per click
  • Golden Click: +500 cookies per klik

Buildings

  • Grandma: +5 cookies per seconde
  • Farm: +20 cookies per seconde
  • Factory: +100 cookies per seconde
  • Mine: +500 cookies per seconde
  • Bank: +2000 cookies per seconde
  • Temple: +10000 cookies per seconde
  • Wizard Tower: +50000 cookies per seconde
